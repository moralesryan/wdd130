Los Rapids Rafting

The best rafting experience!

Founded with love and a passion for adventure, Los Rapids has been providing unforgettable rafting experiences for generations.

The best rafting company since 1930!

During a cold winter in 1930, a group of friends gathered around a campfire to share stories of their adventures on the river. As they reminisced about their past trips, they realized that there was a need for a rafting company that could provide safe and exciting experiences for people of all ages and skill levels. And so, Los Rapids was born.

One of the founders, the well-known inventor and entrepreneur Joe Mama, was an avid rafter who believed in the transformative power of nature, through rafting, Joe Mama wanted to create a space where people could connect with the outdoors and each other. His vision laid the foundation for Los Rapids, and his legacy continues to inspire our team and customers today.

Los Rapids

At Los Rapids, we are dedicated to providing our customers with the best rafting experience possible. Our mission is to create unforgettable memories while ensuring safety and fun for all skill levels, from beginners to experts. We believe in the power of nature and adventure to bring people together and foster a sense of community.

History

Los Rapids was founded in 1930 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for rafting with others. It all started with a small spark of inspiration during a river trip, where the founders realized the potential for adventure and camaraderie that rafting could bring. Over the years, Los Rapids has grown into a renowned rafting company, known for its commitment to safety, customer satisfaction, and environmental stewardship. One of the founders, the well-known inventor and entrepreneur Joe Mama, was an avid rafter who believed in the transformative power of nature, through rafting, Joe Mama wanted to create a space where people could connect with the outdoors and each other. His vision laid the foundation for Los Rapids, and his legacy continues to inspire our team and customers today.
